What is Art?

Art is a visual experience or object that is created through the expression of skill or imagination. It can also be defined as the quality, production, or expression of what is beautiful, appealing, or of more than ordinary significance.

Abstract Art

Abstract art is a non-representational art form that uses colors, shapes, lines, textures, and gestural marks to express ideas and emotions. Abstract art often challenges the viewer to respond to the piece in a more personal and emotional way.

Why Make Art?

There are countless reasons that motivate the creation of art; some of them are making our surroundings more beautiful; creating records of a specific time, place, person or object; and expressing and communicating ideas. Art is inspiring and stimulating for the human mind.
